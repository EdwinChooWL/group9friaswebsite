FAQ

How do I place an order?
Orders are placed via our website.
Should you have an issue placing an order, you could also reach out to our Customer Service team at +65-4444-6677.
We would be most happy to assist by placing an order via phone for you.

What is the shipping policy?
We aim to deliver your order to you within 1 to 3, or 7 to 14 working days, from the day you receive the Order Shipped email notification.
The delivery time estimate depends on the availability of the items at the warehouse, our courier partners' dispatching schedules and routing, as well as traffic and weather conditions.

What is the return policy?
If you are not 100% satisfied with your purchase, you can return your item to us for a full refund.
Returns must be done within 30 days of receipt, un-used with tags on, in original packaging.

What is the refund policy?
Your returned items, together with your return slip, are usually received and accepted at our warehouse 3-5 working days.
A refund would be issued shortly after we receive your items, according to the preferred refund method indicated on your return slip.
